Our Kids Classes are comprised of two sets of age groups:


  • The Cubs Class - ages 6 - 9

  • The Tigers Class - ages 10 - 13

Not only are the classes broken down into age group, they are also divided into appropriate skill levels. This is so those with similar skill levels may progress and learn adequate to their rank.



Most Classes are divided into the ranks of:
  • Beginner Level - White Belt - Green Belt

  • Intermediate Level - Blue Belt - Brown Belt
